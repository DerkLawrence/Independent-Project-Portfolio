A few things about me

My interests have always been linked to getting to know the innerworkings of things. I like to know the hows and whys of functionality. Coding seemed like a natural interest to pursue since it's something my daily life is inextricably tied to. My interest in technological make-ups began early as I would, to the frustration of my family, take apart any belongings that caught my interest. This lent itself later on to an interest in music and performance. I started working at an early age to fund my new and expensive hobbies and the beginning of my working career altered very little thrughout my life. I found working for restaurants, record stores, and music distributors such as CD Baby served to fund and support my ambitions. It is now, in an era defined by a pandemic that I look elsewhere for a new path to expand my interests and forge new paths for the future.
